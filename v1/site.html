Sint-Andries is a great choice for travelers interested in old-town exploration, city walks and romance.

Set in green surroundings within 2.5 mi from medieval Bruges, the modernly designed B&B Lobelia offers spacious guest rooms with hardwood floors and free access to WiFi and private on-site parking. The property’s host is a certified tour guide and can assist with sightseeing.

Rooms feature garden view and are furnished with a TV with cable channels and a seating area. Coffee and tea facilities are provided. Each room’s bathroom is fitted with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Extras include a desk and a safety deposit box. The daily breakfast buffet at the Lobelia includes several kinds of bread and rolls, fruits, spreads, eggs and cheese. When the weather is nice, guests can take breakfast on the outdoor terrace.

The Belfry of Bruges, the Church of Our Lady and Basilica of the Holy Blood can be reached in a 12-minute drive from the accommodations. Bruges Train Station is 3.2 mi away. It is a 20-minute drive to the Belgian coast.

Please note that this is a nonsmoking accommodations.
